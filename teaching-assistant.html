Teaching Assistant

Forum Asisten (FA) is an organization under the UPT Pengembangan Laboratorium dan Teknologi (PLT) at Universitas AMIKOM Purwokerto. It supports lecturers in practical learning activities while developing members’ technical skills, leadership, responsibility, and teamwork. FA consists of three divisions: Programming, Multimedia, and Networking & Intelligent Systems. Learn more about the Assistant Forum (FA) at AMIKOM Purwokerto University

About the Experience

As a Teaching Assistant at Universitas Amikom Purwokerto, I supported lecturers and students in various Informatics courses by assisting practical learning activities, explaining technical concepts, and providing academic guidance in laboratory sessions. I have contributed to several courses, including Introduction to Multimedia, Computer Networks, .NET Framework Programming, Data Communication, 3D Visualization, Mobile Programming, and Natural Language Processing, as well as other Informatics-related subjects. This experience strengthened my technical knowledge in programming, networking, multimedia, mobile development, data communication, and Artificial Intelligence, while also developing my skills in communication, administration, problem-solving, teamwork, and student support. Through this role, I gained valuable experience in managing learning activities and delivering clear technical information to students with different levels of understanding.
